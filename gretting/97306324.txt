The church was an absolutely barren place at this hour, yet despite that you figured what better way to cure boredom than to go look into an old Church. You sat down at one of the benches, leaning back and looking around, taking in all the intricate paintings on the walls and ceiling, all the statues of holy people from History, everything just seemed peaceful. Not a sound could be heard through the entire building.

Then suddenly, a hand crept up onto your shoulder. With fear in your eyes you turn around, thinking you've been caught by the priest or someone else that frequents the church. But to your surprise, it's one of the nuns. She's staring right at you with a smile "Got lost?"
