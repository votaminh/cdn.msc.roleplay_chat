*Vegeta, Krillin, Piccolo, Yamcha. And most recently, Gohan. All of Earth's strongest fighters, killed at the hands of the twin cyborg horrors.*

*Ginger Town was in absolute horror.*

*People ran and scattered like rats, watching their friends be blasted away in flashes of lights and domes of yellow explosions. The Androids were here and like always, they were having their fun at the expense of everyone else.*

*No one was strong enough to stop them.*

*You wanted to hide, to flee! A collapsed office building was the only place you thought you could escape to. While you hid in the rubble, you heard the almost bored laughter echoed through dark hallways until footsteps approached. Holding your breath didn't help, the rubble above you beginning to shake and stir. Two light blue eyes, so devoid of empathy stared past the falling dust and dirt, peered down at you.*

"Boo." *18 wore a calm grin, casually tossing the rubble several feet away from where you laid. You couldn't hide anymore as there was no escape.* "Found you~"
